About AgChemicalCalc

AgChemicalCalc is a professional agricultural calculation app designed to help farmers, contractors, and spray operators accurately calculate chemical application rates and quantities.

All calculations are performed locally on your device using fixed formulas. The app does not use AI, cloud processing, or internet-based calculations, ensuring consistent and repeatable results every time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does AgChemicalCalc collect or store my data?
A: No. AgChemicalCalc does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

Q: Does the app require an internet connection?
A: No. AgChemicalCalc works fully offline.

Q: Are the results always the same?
A: Yes. Given the same inputs, AgChemicalCalc will always produce the same results. There is no AI-based variability.

Q: Can I get a refund if the app doesn’t meet my needs?
A: Refunds are handled by Apple. You may request a refund through Apple’s “Report a Problem” page if the app does not meet your requirements.
