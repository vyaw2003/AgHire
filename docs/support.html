FAQ

Q: How do I create a listing?
A: Tap "Create New Listing", fill in the required fields, add images, and submit.

Q: Can I use AgHire outside Australia?
A: Not yet — currently AgHire only supports Australian listings.

Q: How can I advertise?
A: AgHire is accepting advertisers. Contact us for details.

Q: How can I delete my account and listings?

  • To pause your listing: Login to the app and pause the listing directly.
  • To delete your account:
    • Open the app.
    • Go to Main → Account Management → Delete My Account.
    • Would you like to delete your listings and account? Ok. Your account has been deleted.
  • Alternatively, you can send a request for an admin to delete your account on your behalf.
