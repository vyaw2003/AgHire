About AgCalc

AgCalc is a simple agricultural calculation app designed to help farmers quickly convert, estimate, yield, seed, storage, delivery times and costs and most importantly calculate when you will finish. Stay tuned for more exciting Ag Calulators to come shortly.

FAQ

Q: Does AgCalc store my data?
A: No. AgCalc does not store personal information or user accounts.

Q: Can I save my calculations?
A: Currently AgCalc is designed for quick, on-the-spot use only. Saved projects may be added in a future update.

Q: Does AgCalc work offline?
A: Basic calculations work offline. Some features may require an internet connection.

Q: Why am I seeing ads?
A: AgCalc displays ads to help cover development and operating costs while keeping the app free to use.
